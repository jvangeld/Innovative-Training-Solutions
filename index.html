Learn to Diagnose Electrical Problems

Automotive and Diesel electrical diagnosis trips up a lot of technicians. In fact, it is the most often cited short-coming that technicians admit they have. At the same time, Sean simplifies the concepts and brings creative problem solving to this subject. Now, it is easier to learn how to diagnose common electrical problems using his unique approach to Ohm's Law and no-number math. This is borne out by techs who have taken his courses that are based on these concepts. And in truly amazing fashion, Sean has used these concepts to train employees with no automotive or diesel experience to diagnose HVAC systems and trailer wiring issues on semi-trucks within two weeks and successfully sent them out with their own service truck after that. If they can learn electrical diagnosis, then you can as well!
